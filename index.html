Enderase is youth association that is formed by the youth in the youth also for the youth,with the aim of being light Renaissance for our generation.Enderase has three main programs enlightenment,empowerment and so called exposure the untapped potential of the youths across our country & continent.

Enderase now focus on Enlightenment Program in which opens the eyes of the generation to see light and make change happen, Under Enlightenment we have three inspiring projects namely Dinq Wege Conferences(DWC), The Purpose Tour Trainings (PTT),Giving for Living Charitable works(GLCW) in addition to this Enderase has plans to host tv,radio also social media programs that mainly focus on enlightenment.
